African Institute for Ethical Governance

AIEG delivers an integrated suite of programmes and consulting services designed to foster ethical transformation, institutional reform, and sustainable governance across both public and private sectors.

Services

Public Sector Programmes

Ethical Conduct and Professionalism Cultivation Programme (ECPCP)

ECPCP develops ethics and professionalism within government departments and state entities such as SARS, SAPS, ACSA, and DHA.

Public Service Leadership Integrity Programme (PSLIP)

Develops ethical decision-making and accountability among senior management and state-owned enterprise leaders.

Public Ethics and Governance Improvement Initiative (PEGII)

Supports public institutions in strengthening integrity systems and service delivery ethics.

Private Sector Programmes

Corporate Ethics and Governance Programme (CEGRP)

Empowers boards and executives to integrate ethical governance and ESG compliance.

ESG, Compliance, and Sustainability Leadership Programme (ECSLP)

Equips corporate leaders with the skills to operationalise ethical and sustainable business practices.

Corporate Integrity and Reputation Management (CIRM)

Aligns corporate ethics with brand reputation and stakeholder trust.

Cross-Sector Research and Innovation

African Ethics Observatory

A regional platform tracking governance trends and ethics indicators.

Governance Index

A Benchmarking tool for integrity and accountability maturity.

Ethics Labs & Leadership Dialogues

Co-creation spaces for ethical innovation and thought leadership.
